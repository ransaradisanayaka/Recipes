RECIPES

Nutella Cheesecake

Method 01

INGREDIENTS

Yields: 8-12 slices
    METRICCUPS
  1. 250 grams digestive biscuits
  2. 75 grams soft unsalted butter
  3. 350 grams jar of nutella (at room temperature)
  4. 100 grams chopped toasted hazelnuts
  5. 500 grams cream cheese (at room temperature)
  6. 60 grams icing sugar (sifted)

METHOD

  • Break the digestives into the bowl of a processor, add the butter and a 15ml tablespoon of Nutella, and blitz until it starts to clump. Add 25g/3 tablespoons of the hazelnuts and continue to pulse until you have a damp, sandy mixture.
  • Tip into a 23cm/9inch round springform and press into the base either using your hands or the back of a spoon. Place in the fridge to chill.
  • Beat the cream cheese and icing sugar until smooth and then add the remaining Nutella to the cream cheese mixture, and continue beating until combined. Take the springform out of the fridge and carefully smooth the Nutella mixture over the base. Scatter the remaining chopped hazelnuts on top to cover and place the tin in the fridge for at least four hours or overnight. Serve straight from the fridge for best results

Method 02

INGREDIENTS

    *For the Crust:*
  • *Graham crackers or digestive biscuits*: 1½ cups, crushed
  • *Unsalted butter*: ½ cup, melted
    • *For the Filling:*
  • *Cream cheese*: 2 cups (16 oz), at room temperature
  • *Nutella*: 1½ cups
  • *Powdered sugar*: ½ cup
  • *Vanilla extract*: 1 teaspoon
  • *Whipping cream*: 1 cup
    • *For Topping:*
  • *Whipped cream*: for garnish (optional)
  • *Chocolate shavings or chopped hazelnuts*: for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. 1. *Prepare the crust*: In a medium bowl, combine the crushed graham crackers or digestive biscuits with the melted butter. Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan. Chill in the refrigerator while you prepare the filling.
  2. *Make the filling*: In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth and creamy. Add the Nutella and powdered sugar, and continue to beat until fully combined and smooth. Add the vanilla extract.
  3. *Whip the cream*: In a separate bowl, whip the cream until stiff peaks form. Gently fold the whipped cream into the Nutella mixture until well combined.
  4. *Assemble the cheesecake*: Pour the Nutella filling over the chilled crust and spread it evenly with a spatula.
  5. *Chill*: Refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4-6 hours or overnight until it’s set.
  6. *Serve*: Before serving, remove the cheesecake from the springform pan. Garnish with whipped cream, chocolate shavings, or chopped hazelnuts if desired.

Method 03

INGREDIENTS

    For the Crust:*
  • Oreo cookies*: 1½ cups, crushed
  • Unsalted butter*: ¼ cup, melted
    • *For the Filling:*
  • *Cream cheese*: 3 cups (24 oz), at room temperature
  • *Granulated sugar*: ¾ cup
  • *Nutella*: 1½ cups
  • *Vanilla extract*: 1 teaspoon
  • *Eggs*: 3 large
  • *Sour cream*: ½ cup
    • *For Topping:*
  • - *Nutella*: ¼ cup, melted for drizzling (optional)
  • *Chopped hazelnuts*: for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. *Preheat the oven*: Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. *Prepare the crust*: In a medium bowl, mix the crushed Oreo cookies with the melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of the springform pan to form an even crust. Bake for 8-10 minutes, then set aside to cool.
  3. *Make the filling*: In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar until smooth and creamy. Add the Nutella and vanilla extract, and continue to beat until well combined.
  4. *Add the eggs*: Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Then, add the sour cream and mix until fully incorporated.
  5. *Bake the cheesecake*: Pour the filling over the cooled crust and smooth the top with a spatula. Bake the cheesecake for 50-60 minutes, or until the center is set but still slightly jiggly.
  6. *Cool*: Turn off the oven and crack the oven door slightly, allowing the cheesecake to cool gradually for about an hour. This helps prevent cracking. After that, remove the cheesecake from the oven and let it cool to room temperature.
  7. *Chill*: Refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  8. *Serve*: Before serving, drizzle melted Nutella over the top and sprinkle with chopped hazelnuts if desired. Slice and enjoy your baked Nutella cheesecake!
