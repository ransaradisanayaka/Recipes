RECIPES

Potato Snacks

Method 01

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 potato, boiled & grated
  • ½ tsp ginger garlic paste
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  • 3 tbsp cheddar cheese, grated
  • ½ tsp mixed herbs
  • ¼ tsp pepper, crushed
  • salt to taste
  • ½ tsp chaat masala
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves, finely chopped
  • ¼ cup bread crumbs
  • ¼ cup corn flour
  • ¼ tsp pepper, crushed
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ cup water
  • oil for deep frying

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Firstly, in a large mixing bowl take 2 potato, 3 tbsp cheddar cheese, ½ tsp ginger garlic paste, ½ tsp chilli flakes, ½ tsp mixed herbs, ¼ tsp pepper, ½ tsp chaat masala, ¼ tsp salt and 2 tbsp coriander leaves.
  2. Mix well making sure the spices are combined well. Now add ¼ cup bread crumbs and combine well. breadcrumbs helps to absorb moisture and prevent from breaking in oil.
  3. Further prepare corn flour batter by mixing ¼ cup corn flour, ¼ tsp pepper, ¼ tsp salt and ¼ cup water. Prepare a smooth flowing consistency batter. Pinch a small ball sized potato dough and roll to cylindrical shape.
  4. Dip in corn flour batter covering all sides. Then roll in bread crumbs.
  5. Deep fry, shallow fry or pan fry the patties in hot oil. alternatively, preheat and bake at 180 degree celsius for 25 minutes.
  6. Flip and fry all the sides on medium flame till it turns golden and crisp.
  7. Finally, drain over kitchen paper to remove excess oil and serve potato nuggets with tomato sauce.

Method 02

INGREDIENTS

  • *Potatoes*: 4 large, washed and unpeeled
  • *Olive oil*: 2-3 tablespoons
  • Garlic powder*: 1 teaspoon
  • Paprika*: 1 teaspoon
  • Dried oregano*: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt*: to taste
  • Black pepper*: to taste
  • Parmesan cheese*: 2 tablespoons, grated (optional)
  • Fresh parsley*: chopped, for garnish

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. *Preheat the oven*: Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. *Cut the potatoes*: Slice the potatoes into wedges by cutting each potato into halves and then each half into quarters.
  3. *Season the wedges*: In a large bowl, toss the potato wedges with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. If using, add the Parmesan cheese for extra flavor.
  4. *Bake*: Arrange the seasoned potato wedges on a baking sheet in a single layer. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the wedges are golden brown and crispy.
  5. *Garnish and serve*: Once baked, remove the wedges from the oven and sprinkle with fresh parsley. Serve with ketchup, sour cream, or your favorite dipping sauce.

Method 03

INGREDIENTS

  • *Potatoes*: 4 medium, boiled and mashed
  • *Cheddar cheese*: ½ cup, grated
  • *Cornflour*: 2 tablespoons
  • *Green chilies*: 1-2, finely chopped
  • *Coriander leaves*: 2 tablespoons, finely chopped
  • *Salt*: to taste
  • *Black pepper*: to taste
  • *Breadcrumbs*: 1 cup
  • *Egg*: 1, beaten (or ¼ cup milk for a vegetarian option)
  • *Oil*: for deep frying

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. *Prepare the potato mixture*: In a large bowl, combine the mashed potatoes, grated cheese, cornflour, green chilies, coriander leaves, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until the ingredients are thoroughly combined.
  2. *Shape the balls*: Take small portions of the potato mixture and roll them into balls. You can make them bite-sized or slightly larger, depending on your preference.
  3. *Coat the balls*: Dip each ball into the beaten egg (or milk), then roll it in breadcrumbs to coat evenly.
  4. *Chill*: Place the coated potato balls in the refrigerator for about 15-20 minutes. This helps them firm up and prevents them from breaking apart while frying.
  5. *Fry the balls*: Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, fry the potato balls in batches until they are golden brown and crispy. Make sure to turn them occasionally for even frying.
  6. *Drain and serve*: Remove the fried potato balls with a slotted spoon and drain them on paper towels to remove excess oil. Serve hot with ketchup, chili sauce, or any dip of your choice.
