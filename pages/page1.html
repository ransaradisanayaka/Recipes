RECIPES

Veg Sandwich



Method 01

Ingredients

  1. 6 slices bread
  2. butter to toast
    3. For vegetable filling:
  3. 1 teaspoon garlic crushed
  4. 1 medium sized onion finely chopped
  5. 2 small carrots finely chopped
  6. 1/2 small capsicum finely chopped
  7. 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  8. 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  9. 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  10. salt to taste

Instructions

  • Heat oil in a pan - add crushed garlic.
  • Add carrot and capsicum.
  • Saute until it shrinks and raw smell leaves.
  • Add red chilli powder and garam masala powder
  • Give a quick saute.
  • Add mayo.
  • Mix it well. Switch off
  • Add generous filling to a bread slice.
  • Seal it with another slice.
  • Heat butter on a dosa tawa. Place the prepared sandwich. Press with dosa flipper. Brush with butter on top.
  • Flip over and toast until golden brown on both the sides.
  • Remove and place on chopping board, slice into half and serve Veg Sandwich hot!

Metho 02

Ingredients

  • Bread slices*: 4 (whole wheat or white)
  • Butter or mayonnaise*: 2 tablespoons
  • Cucumber*: 1, thinly sliced
  • Tomato*: 1, thinly sliced
  • Onion*: 1 small, thinly sliced
  • Carrot*: 1 small, grated
  • Capsicum (bell pepper)*: ½, thinly sliced
  • Lettuce leaves*: 2 (optional)
  • Cheese slice*: 2 (optional)
  • Salt*: to taste
  • Black pepper*: to taste
  • Chaat masala*: a pinch (optional)
  • Green chutney*: 2 tablespoons (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prep the bread*: Spread butter or mayonnaise on one side of each bread slice.
  2. Layer the vegetables*: On the buttered side of two slices, spread a thin layer of green chutney if using. Layer the cucumber, tomato, onion, carrot, capsicum, and lettuce on top.
  3. Season*: Sprinkle a little salt, black pepper, and chaat masala over the vegetables.
  4. Add cheese*: If using, place a slice of cheese on top of the vegetables.
  5. Close the sandwich*: Place the remaining two bread slices on top, buttered side down, to close the sandwiches.
  6. Grill or toast*: You can eat the sandwich as is, or grill/toast it until golden and crispy.
  7. Serve*: Cut the sandwich diagonally and serve with ketchup or more green chutney.

Metho 03

Ingredients

  • Bread slices*: 4 (whole wheat or white)
  • Butter*: 2 tablespoons
  • Mayonnaise or cream cheese*: 2 tablespoons
  • Cucumber*: 1, thinly sliced
  • Tomato*: 1, thinly sliced
  • Onion*: 1 small, thinly sliced
  • Carrot*: 1 small, grated
  • Capsicum (bell pepper)*: ½, thinly sliced
  • Boiled potato*: 1, thinly sliced
  • Lettuce leaves*: 2 (optional)
  • Cheese slice*: 2 (optional)
  • Green chutney*: 2 tablespoons
  • Tomato ketchup*: 1 tablespoon
  • Salt*: to taste
  • Black pepper*: to taste
  • Chaat masala*: a pinch
  • Red chili flakes*: a pinch (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the bread*: Lightly toast the bread slices on a pan or toaster until just golden.
  2. Spread butter*: Once the bread is toasted, spread a thin layer of butter on each slice.
  3. Spread condiments*: On two of the buttered slices, spread a layer of mayonnaise or cream cheese. On the other two slices, spread a layer of green chutney.
  4. Layer the vegetables*: - Start with a layer of cucumber slices. - Add a layer of boiled potato slices. - Follow with tomato slices, onion slices, grated carrot, and capsicum slices. - Place a lettuce leaf on top of the vegetables if using.
  5. Add seasoning*: Sprinkle salt, black pepper, chaat masala, and red chili flakes (if using) evenly over the vegetables.
  6. Add cheese*: Place a slice of cheese on top of the vegetables on the slices with mayonnaise or cream cheese.
  7. Close the sandwich*: Place the slices with green chutney on top of the ones with mayonnaise and vegetables, chutney side down, to form two sandwiches.
  8. Grill or toast (optional)*: If you prefer a warm sandwich, grill or toast the sandwich in a sandwich maker or on a pan until the bread is crispy and the cheese is melted.
  9. Serve*: Cut the sandwiches diagonally and serve with tomato ketchup or extra green chutney on the side.
